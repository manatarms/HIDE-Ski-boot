Uploading data

Click the Import Data button. Choose the video file and the CSV files for the left and right foot. Click the Load Jump button.
[Picture example]

Synchronize Video

Use the pressure graph scroll bar to find the jump moment. Find the corresponding jump moment in the video. There is a drop-off in pressure just after the skier leaves the ground, so that would be an easy place to synch. Click the Synchronize button.
[Picture example]


Test the synchronization by choosing a spot in the video and pressing play. If the video does not sync up with the data as expected, uncheck the Synchronize button and choose a new place to synchronize.

Saving Comments

Enter comments in the comments text box. Click the Save button to save the comments.
